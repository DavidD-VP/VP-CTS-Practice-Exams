CTS Practice Exams

The purpose of these exams are to help you prepare for the CTS certification exam. The questions from these exams are the chapter review questions from the official CTS Exam Guide book. Select a desired exam and then press the take exam button to begin. You may select a specific chapter exam or take an exam with all of the questions from each chapter known as the Book exam. At the end of the exam you will be given your score results along with the correct answers to the questions answered incorrectly.
